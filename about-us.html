ABOUT DDP

Diaspora Dream Project

The Diaspora Dream Project is wholly black owned Real Estate and Land Education Endeavour brought to you by the Land and Homes Consult and its partners.

Land and Homes Consult is a land management and real Estate company that focuses on providing serene serviced plots and affordable homes to our would-be clients and their families.

We go further to assist with our unparalleled Building Support Services.

The Diaspora Dream Project is a special real estate endeavour tailored to cater for the needs of Africans and other interested persons abroad. The race is on to get Africa on par with the rest of the world.
The Diaspora Dream Project is made up of the Diaspora Dream Homes, The Diaspora Dream Play-To-Lear, and The Diaspora Serviced Communities.

The project provides special services to Africans in the diaspora to help them acquire parcels of land for various developments such as residential homes, hospitals, commercial ventures, and charity.

The pilot project is set in the Kumasi, the Garden City of Ghana.
